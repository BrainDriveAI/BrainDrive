# Saved Budget Rules

Use this when deciding whether `../budget.md` can be changed.

## Default

`../budget.md` is the saved monthly spending plan. Preserve it unless the owner is creating, revising, or explicitly approving changes to the saved plan.

## Safe Writes To `budget.md`

You may write `../budget.md` when:

- the owner asks to create a first budget,
- the owner asks to revise category limits,
- the owner asks to add, remove, or rename saved categories,
- the owner approves recommended budget changes,
- the owner asks to update fixed bills or budget goals.

## Read-Only For Comparison

During saved-budget comparison mode, `../budget.md` is read-only.

Requests that make it read-only for the turn include:

- "how did I do?"
- "compare this month"
- "am I over budget?"
- "leave the saved budget alone"
- "do not rewrite the saved budget"
- "compare against the saved budget"

If comparison reveals that the saved plan should change, list recommendations in `../reports/latest.md` under `Next Actions` and wait for the owner to ask for a budget revision.

## Category Stability

Keep categories stable because reports and rules refer to them. Add, remove, or rename categories only when the owner understands the change and it matches how they want to think about spending.
